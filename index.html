Home or homepage is the first part of a website page that contains information in the form of a greeting, introduction, brief description, website status, menu list, and logo.

the "about" section is the section that contains the website owner's profile. This section can contain brief information such as name, origin or address, contact, expertise, products and offers, skills, curriculum vitae, history, and so on.

the "services" section is the section that contains all the offers you provide to your website visitors. The offer can be in the form of services or products. this section is usually used by people who have certain skills that he can offer to others. for that this section is an optional section only and can be used if needed.

the "portfolio" section is the section that displays the works or projects that you have completed with the intent and purpose for website visitors to know the results of your work.

the "footer" or "contact" section is the bottom part of the website page which contains information in the form of address, location, contact, links, menu list, logo, and comment form.
